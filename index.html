About The Studio

Personal. Original. Uncompromising.

It began in the quiet lanes of Chellikere, a small village where Mohammed Faizan learned that the most memorable celebrations aren't born from big budgets — they're built from heart, hustle, and an obsessive eye for detail. What started as helping friends and family plan intimate gatherings soon became a calling.

Word spread. One event led to another. From village courtyards to city banquet halls, Faizan's reputation grew — not through marketing, but through the one thing that never lies: client satisfaction. Every smile, every tear of joy, every guest who said "this was the best event we've ever attended" became the foundation of FZ Eventz.

Today, the same village boy runs a full-fledged architectural event production studio from Hebbal Kempapura, Bangalore. But the philosophy remains unchanged — we don't sell packages, we engineer memories. Every event is personal, every detail matters, and every client is family.

— Mohammed Faizan, Founder

Based out of Hebbal Kempapura, our specialized team designs environments that mirror your personality, heritage, and style. We control every logistical element from conceptualization to execution, ensuring you remain a guest at your own celebration.
